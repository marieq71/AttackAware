Ransomware

What is Ransomware?

Ransomware is a type of malicious software designed to block access to a computer system until a sum of money is paid. It typically encrypts the user's data, making it inaccessible.

How to Prevent Ransomware?

  • Regularly update your software and operating systems.
  • Use strong passwords and multi-factor authentication.
  • Back up your data frequently.
  • Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading suspicious files.
